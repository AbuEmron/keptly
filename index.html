Finding your way

Navigating Keptly takes about a minute.

01

The bar at the bottom

Five core areas live on the tab bar — Today, Goals, Projects, Vault and Finance. On desktop they're in the left sidebar, along with Home, Vehicles and Family.

02

The + button adds anything

The floating button always adds whatever fits the screen you're on — a goal on Goals, a task inside a project, a document in the Vault.

03

Search finds it instantly

The magnifier in the top bar searches across every module at once — a task, a policy number, a person, a bill.

04

Settings holds your account

The gear icon opens Settings: download a backup, take the guided tour, and under Account & Sync, start Premium to sync across devices.

05

Emergency is always one tap

The red button in the corner opens Emergency Mode — contacts, medical info and your family plan — even with no signal.
